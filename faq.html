FAQ’S

Shipping

  • How long after order placement will the product ship?

    We usually ship the next business day after payment has cleared.

  • What shipping service will it ship from?

    We ship using the best method of delivery for each order via major carriers.

  • Will we ship to an address different from the Billing Address?

    In most cases yes, but we may need to call and verify the shipping address in certain circumstances. Adult signature may be required for some shipments.

  • Where do you ship to?

    We ship where we are able to ship in compliance with government regulations. Domestic USA and also international. If you have a question, please email us to verify. Adult signature may be required for some shipments.
